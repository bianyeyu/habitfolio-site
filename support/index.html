HabitFolio Support

HabitFolio is a local-first habit tracker for iPhone and iPad. Each habit becomes a book, each day becomes a page, and your progress becomes chapters you can revisit or export.

Common Questions

Where is my data stored?

HabitFolio stores data on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If iCloud sync is available for your Apple account, SwiftData may mirror your data through Apple's CloudKit service so it can sync across your devices.

Does HabitFolio require an account?

No. HabitFolio does not operate its own account system. iCloud sync, if available, uses your Apple account.

Can I use HabitFolio without notifications?

Yes. Reminders are optional. You can track habits, write notes, and use widgets without enabling notifications. PDF export is available with HabitFolio Pro and does not require notification access.

Does HabitFolio upload my habit content to generate PDFs?

No. PDF export is a HabitFolio Pro feature generated on device.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete habits and entries inside the app. You can also delete the app to remove local app data from the device. If data has synced to iCloud, retention and deletion may also depend on your Apple account, iCloud settings, backups, and Apple's sync behavior.
